Popular Destinations

Munnar

Munnar, nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India, is renowned for its lush tea gardens, rolling hills, and cool climate. This charming hill station attracts visitors with its scenic beauty, rich biodiversity, and attractions like the Eravikulam National Park and Anamudi Peak.

Kedarnath

Kedarnath is a revered pilgrimage site in Uttarakhand, India, nestled in the Himalayas at an altitude of 3,583 meters. Home to the ancient Kedarnath Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, it attracts thousands of devotees and trekkers each year, offering stunning views of snow-capped peaks and serene landscapes.

Goa

Goa, located on the southwestern coast of India, is renowned for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage architecture. It attracts tourists with its relaxed atmosphere, water sports, cultural festivals, and delicious seafood, making it a popular destination for both domestic and international travelers seeking sun, sand, and culture.

Featured Hotels

Elixir Hills

Elixir Hills is a luxury resort in Munnar, Kerala, set amidst lush green forests and tea plantations. Known for its elegant suites, eco-friendly amenities, and breathtaking views, the resort offers a serene retreat with opportunities for nature walks, wildlife sightings, and relaxation in a tranquil environment.

Kedarayatih Resort

Kedarayatih Resort is a comfortable accommodation option in Kedarnath, Uttarakhand, offering a peaceful stay amidst the stunning Himalayan landscape. Located close to the sacred Kedarnath Temple, it provides modern amenities and cozy rooms, making it a convenient and serene retreat for pilgrims and nature enthusiasts alike.

Beleza By The Beach

Beleza By The Beach is a luxury resort located in Goa, offering a tranquil beachfront experience with modern amenities, spacious accommodations, and scenic views of the Arabian Sea. Known for its serene ambiance and hospitality, it provides a perfect blend of relaxation and luxury for visitors seeking a rejuvenating getaway in Goa.

Featured Activities

Eravikulam National Park Safari

Eravikulam National Park in Munnar, Kerala, offers safaris showcasing its lush grasslands, scenic beauty, and the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, providing a unique opportunity to experience the rich biodiversity of the Western Ghats.

Trekking to Kedarnath Peak

Trekking to Kedarnath Peak, one of the highest peaks in the Gangotri Group of Garhwal Himalayas, offers adventurers breathtaking views and a challenging climb amidst stunning alpine landscapes and glaciers.

Parasiling

Parasailing in Goa offers an exhilarating experience as you soar above the Arabian Sea, enjoying panoramic views of the coastline and feeling the rush of wind against the parachute. It's a popular water sport for thrill-seekers and those looking to combine adventure with scenic beauty.
